Minimal Text #1

Full image web template

For pages light in text

A design for webpages that are light on text, or that 'open up' in their subpages. Background could be an image or video. Resizing to mobile creates a "two-tone" effect, splitting the page into an image section and a scrollable text section.

Background photo by Mary Runge.
